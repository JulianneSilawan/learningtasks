Possible strategies to help bring down poverty in the Philippines

- Ways to help reduce poverty -

    Increase of employment

    The increasing of employment will then help the poor get back up again and will be able to find a good shelter for their families.

    Government support in Health, Education, and Infrastracture

    With the help of the government, the poor will have a better oppportunity especially in getting a educational support since it will give them a better future.

    Increase of Monthly Salary Pay

    The increase of salary pay will help them buy their monthly dues like their groceries and they will not suffer from hunger again.

Further goals and key strategies?

One of the key strategies to help bring down poverty in the Philippines is providing birth control to the poor. In a radical move for the heavily populated Catholic country, the President made readily available birth control to nearly 6 million women who cannot afford it.

Next, the goal for the Philippine government is to create more jobs, improve productivity, invest in health and nutrition while focusing on reducing poverty. If the government is able to execute its plans successfully, it is capable of reducing poverty in the Philippines by 13 to 15 percent by 2022.

How can we reduce poverty in our society?

Aid and government support in health, education, and infrastructure helps growth by increasing human and physical capital. Poverty alleviation also involves improving the living conditions of people who are already poor.
