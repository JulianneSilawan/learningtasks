Vegetarian Rice

Ingredients

  • 1/2 chopped onion
  • 1 chopped garlic clove
  • 1 chopped courgette
  • 1 grated carrot
  • 1 chopped red or yellow pepper
  • 75g frozen peas
  • 1/2 tin of chopped tomatoes
  • 350ml Vegetable stock
  • 150g Dried brown rice
  • 1 tbsp of dried parsely
  • 1 tsp tumeric
  • Seasoning

    • Method

    Serves 2: Prep time 10 mins: cook time 25 min

    1. Add onion, garlic and tumeric to pan with a splash of vegetable stock
    2. Stirring, cook gently for 3 mins then add thr chopped courgette, grated carrot and chopped pepper
    3. Stir well for a further 3 mins then add the dried rice
    4. Stir rice in well with vegetables
    5. Once rice is distributed well throughout the mixture add the stock - leave to boil
      6. .
    6. Add in chopped tomatoes and frozen peas and stir
    7. Cover and simmer for a further 10 minutes
    8. Once cooked stir in dried parsely with seasoning
    9. Sppon ricce into 2 dishes
