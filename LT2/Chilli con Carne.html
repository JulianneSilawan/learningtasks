Chilli con Carne

This was one of the first easy recipes I learnt to cook and can be mastered in just a few attempts! It's healthy, cheap and tastey and makes the ultimate student food. You can make a large batch of this and then store in the fridge for days or freeze for weeks.

Ingredients

  • Ground/minced beef 500g
  • 1 Large onion chopped
  • 2-3 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1-2 Tins of chopped tomatoes 400g
  • Squeeze of tomato puree
  • 1 teaspoon of chilli poweder cumin
  • 1 teaspoon of ground cumin
  • dash of Worcester sauce
  • Sprinkle of salt and pepper
  • 1 Chopped red pepper
  • 1 tin of drained kidney beans 400g

Methods

  1. Fry the onion in a hot pan with oil nearly brown then add chopped garlic
  2. Add the mince and stir unti; brown-drain any excess fat if desired
  3. Add all the dried spices and seasoning then reduce heat and add chopped tomatoes
  4. Stir well and add tomato puree and worcester sauce then leave to simmer for about an hour (less if you're in a rush)
  5. Add the chopped red pepper and continue to simmer for 5 mins, then add the tin of drained kideny beans and cook for further 5 mins. If the chilli become to dry at any point just add a bit of water.
  6. Serve with rice, jacket potatoes or pasta!
