Pineapple Chicken

4 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp canola or sesame oil
  • 1 (10-oz) package frozen broccoli (or stir-fry vegetable mix), thewed*
  • 1/2 C stir-fry sauce
  • 1/4 C pineapple juice, use reserved juice from canned pineapple
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper (optional - use if you like it hot!)
  • 1 (15-oz) can pineapple chunks or tidbits, drained (sereserve the juice!)
  • 2 C diced cooked chicken or 2 (10-oz) cans chicken breast, drained or flaked

    • Method

    1. Heat the oil in large skillet over medium high heat. Add all ingredients EXCEPT the pineapple & chicken. Cook and stir unitl heated through, 5-6 min.
    2. Add pineapple and chicken; cook another 2 minutes.
    3. Serve over instant brown rice or whole wheat pasta.
    4. *Thaw frozen vegetables in the microwave or by holding the package under cold running water for several minutes.
