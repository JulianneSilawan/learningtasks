Pad Thai Chicken

Ingredients

  • Essential items:
  • Rice noodles (can use another type of noodle but read packet instructions for how to cook)
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • salt and pepper
  • 3 medium red chillis (chopped)
  • 3 spring onions (chopped)
  • 2 eggs
  • Corriander ( if i odn;t have any corriander I substitute for dome dried mint)
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 3 tbsp of fish sauce

    • The following ingredients aren't essential but if you can afford them they taste fantastic in this dish

  • 60g cooked shrimp
  • 3 tbsp roasted peanuts (slightlu ground)
  • beansprouts
  • mange tout or sugar snap peas

    • Method

    1. Soak the noodles in hot water for 20 minutes or until cooked
    2. Heat some oil in a pan and add the two beaten eggs and seasoned eggs to make an omelette. Transfer to a plate and chop into strips and leave to cool.
    3. Heat some oil in the work and add the chicken, once it is sealed on all sides add the garlic.
    4. Add the spring onions and shrimp (if using) and stir fry for another minute
    5. Add thr chillis, drained noodles, fish sauce, lemon juice, sugar, and peanuts and stir fry over a high heat for another minute.
    6. Toss in the beansprouts, omelette strips, mange tout and stir fry briskly for about 30 seconds.
    7. Toss over corriander and season to taste. Serve piping hot.
